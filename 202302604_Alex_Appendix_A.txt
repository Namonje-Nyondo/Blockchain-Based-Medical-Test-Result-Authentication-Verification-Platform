PROJECT SUMMARY

Project Title: MedVerify Blockchain Medical Verification Platform

Problem Statement:
Traditional centralized medical document verification systems are prone to:
- Delays in verification processes
- Lack of transparency in document audit trails
- Vulnerability to unauthorized access
- Inability for patients to control their own medical records

Solution:
A decentralized blockchain-based platform enabling:
- Secure, tamper-proof medical document registration
- Transparent verification with immutable audit trails
- Role-based access control (Labs, Verifiers, Patients)
- Patient control of their medical records
- Cryptographic verification using hashes

Blockchain Network: Local Ethereum (Hardhat)
Smart Contract: MedVerifyRegistry.sol (Solidity 0.8.24)
Frontend: React + Vite (JavaScript)
Wallet Integration: MetaMask + ethers.js v6
